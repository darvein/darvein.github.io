## General Basic Roadmap
Consider these points:
- Greetings
- Self introductions
- Grammar Essentials
    - Tenses
    - Adjetives
    - Adverbs
    - Prepositions
    - Verbs (Etre, Avoir, Aller, Faire and Conjugations)
- Questions (qui, quoi, Ou, Pourquoi, Comment)
- Alphabet, Numbers
- Time and Space vocabulary
    - Date and time
    - Places of objects?
- Daily Routines
