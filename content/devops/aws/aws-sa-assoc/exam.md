## Solutions Architect Associate

General knowledge from AWS needed:

- Network:
  - VPC
  - Route53
  - Migrations
    - Direct connect
    - Snowball, edge, snowmobile
    - Data sync
- Compute:
  - EC2, ECS
  - Queues: SQS, SNF
  - High availability
  - Serverless
  - Caching: CDN
- Database: RDS
- Storage: EFS, EBS, S3
- Users (users, roles, policies)
- NoSQL:
  - Dynamodb
  - Athena
  - Redshift
  - Elasticsearch?
